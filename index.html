Yunpeng Chen (陈云鹏)

I'm Yunpeng CHEN, a final year PhD student at National University of Singapore (NUS), advised by Assistant Professor Jiashi Feng and Associate Professor Shuicheng Yan. I earned my bachelor's degree from Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST) in 2015, advised by Associate Professor Fuhao Zou.

I am currently a summer research intern at Facebook AML working with Yannis Kalantidis, Zhicheng Yan and Marcus Rohrbach. Before that, I did an summer internship at The University of Hong Kong (HKU) with Professor Wenping Wang in 2014.

Research Interest

My general research interests lie at the intersection of deep learning and computer vision.

Recently, I focus on:

  • Video understanding and representation learning
  • Deep learning architecture engineering
