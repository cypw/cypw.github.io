Yunpeng Chen 陈云鹏

I am currently a PhD student at National University of Singapore (NUS), advised by Assistant Professor Jiashi Feng and Associate Professor Shuicheng Yan. I earned my bachelor's degree from Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST) in 2015, advised by Associate Professor Fuhao Zou. In summer 2014, I did an internship in The University of Hong Kong (HKU) with Professor Wenping Wang.

  chenyunpeng AT u DOT nus DOT edu                               Google Scholar

Research Interest

My general research interests lie at the intersection of deep learning and computer vision.

Recently, I focus on:

  • Deep learning for real-time object detection, including 2D object detection and 3D object detection
  • Deep learning architecture engineering
